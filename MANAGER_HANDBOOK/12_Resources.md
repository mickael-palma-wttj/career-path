Curated list of books, tools, frameworks, and communities to enhance your management skills and IC career development practice.

## üìö Recommended Books

### Essential Management Books

**"The Manager's Path" by Camille Fournier**
- The definitive guide to engineering management
- Covers IC to CTO journey
- Practical advice at every level
- **Best for:** Understanding management career path and responsibilities

**"Radical Candor" by Kim Scott**
- Framework for giving effective feedback
- Care personally + Challenge directly
- Concrete examples and tools
- **Best for:** Improving feedback and communication

**"An Elegant Puzzle" by Will Larson**
- Systems thinking for engineering management
- Organizational design and scaling
- Career development frameworks
- **Best for:** Senior managers and organizational leaders

**"The Culture Code" by Daniel Coyle**
- Building high-performing teams
- Psychological safety and belonging
- Culture creation and maintenance
- **Best for:** Team building and culture development

### Career Development Focused

**"Mindset" by Carol Dweck**
- Growth mindset vs. fixed mindset
- Implications for development and coaching
- Research-backed frameworks
- **Best for:** Understanding how people grow and develop

**"Thanks for the Feedback" by Douglas Stone & Sheila Heen**
- Receiving and processing feedback
- Understanding defensive reactions
- Growth through feedback
- **Best for:** Helping ICs receive feedback better

**"Peak" by Anders Ericsson**
- Science of deliberate practice
- Skill acquisition and mastery
- Designing effective development activities
- **Best for:** Creating effective development plans

### Performance Management

**"Crucial Conversations" by Patterson, Grenny, et al.**
- High-stakes communication
- Managing difficult conversations
- Achieving mutual purpose
- **Best for:** Handling conflict and difficult situations

**"Powerful" by Patty McCord**
- Netflix culture of freedom and responsibility
- Candid feedback and high performance
- Radical transparency
- **Best for:** Performance-focused culture building

### Leadership & Influence

**"Multipliers" by Liz Wiseman**
- How great leaders amplify others
- Accidental diminisher patterns
- Building capability in teams
- **Best for:** Developing leadership at all levels

**"Turn the Ship Around!" by L. David Marquet**
- Intent-based leadership
- Distributing control and decision-making
- Building leaders at every level
- **Best for:** Empowering ICs and building autonomy

## üí° Creating Your Learning Plan

### For New Managers

**Months 1-3:**
1. Read: "The Manager's Path"
2. Take: Management Fundamentals course
3. Join: Rands Leadership Slack
4. Find: Mentor or peer group

**Months 4-6:**
1. Read: "Radical Candor"
2. Practice: Weekly 1:1s and feedback
3. Attend: Local management meetup
4. Start: Building your own resources

**Months 7-12:**
1. Read: "An Elegant Puzzle"
2. Attend: Management conference
3. Develop: Your management philosophy
4. Share: What you've learned

### For Experienced Managers

**Continuous Learning:**
- Read 1-2 management books per quarter
- Attend 1-2 conferences per year
- Maintain active community membership
- Mentor newer managers
- Write and share your own insights
