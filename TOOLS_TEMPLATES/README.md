### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Development_Plan_Template.md
**Purpose:** Document IC's career goals and development priorities for a set period (usually 6-12 months)

**When to use:** After 1:1 career conversation, beginning of each quarter or year

**What's included:**
- Current role and level
- Career goals (short and long-term)
- Development priorities (top 2-3 areas to focus on)
- Specific actions and learning opportunities
- Success metrics (how will we know progress?)
- Timeline and milestones
- Support and resources needed

**Why it helps:** Creates accountability and clarity about what success looks like

**‚Üí [Use: Development Plan Template](./Development_Plan_Template.md)**

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ Goal_Setting_Template.md
**Purpose:** Framework for setting quarterly or annual goals with ICs

**When to use:** Beginning of quarter (Q1) or year, with check-ins mid-way through

**What's included:**

### 7Ô∏è‚É£ PIP_Template.md
**Purpose:** Performance Improvement Plan structure when IC is underperforming

**When to use:** After coaching attempts haven't worked, you need formal documented improvement plan

**What's included:**
- Clear description of performance issue
- Specific behaviors that need to change

- Goals for improvement with metrics
- Support and resources IC will receive
- Consequences if improvement isn't achieved
- Check-in schedule
- Documentation

**Why it helps:** Creates clear expectations and documented process for managing underperformance

### 9Ô∏è‚É£ Promotion_Evaluation_Checklist.md
**Purpose:** Step-by-step checklist for evaluating IC's readiness for promotion

**When to use:** Before deciding to promote someone

**What's included:**
- Have we observed consistent performance at current level?
- Technical skill assessment
- Soft skills assessment
- Leadership impact assessment
- 360 feedback collection and review
- Peer input and manager consensus
- Career conversation (did they want this?)
- Documentation and evidence
- Final decision checklist

**Why it helps:** Ensures you've done thorough due diligence before promoting, reduces bias

**‚Üí [Use: Promotion Evaluation Checklist](./Promotion_Evaluation_Checklist.md)**

### üîü Promotion_Packet_Template.md

## üí° How to Use These Templates

### 1. **Copy & Customize**
- These are starting points, not gospel
- Adapt language to your team and company culture
- Add or remove sections as needed
- Make them your own

### 2. **Share with ICs**
- Consider sharing development plans and goal-setting templates with ICs so they can co-create
- This increases buy-in and ownership

### 3. **Keep for Documentation**
- Save completed versions in your records (with appropriate privacy)
- These become part of IC's file and promotion documentation
- Useful for showing patterns over time

### 4. **Refer to Examples**
- Read the related guides and case studies for context on how to use each template
- Each template has companion guidance in PRACTICAL_GUIDES or MANAGER_HANDBOOK

### 5. **Iterate**
- What works at your company may be different than what works elsewhere
- Get feedback from your team on what's useful
- Modify templates based on what you learn
