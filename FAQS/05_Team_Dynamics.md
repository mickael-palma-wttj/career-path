# Team Dynamics

Questions about managing team relationships and diversity.

---

## Q: How do I handle conflict between two ICs?

**A:** Address early, facilitate resolution.

**Step 1: Understand the Conflict**
- Talk to both parties separately
- Get their perspectives
- Identify root cause (work style? Miscommunication? Values clash?)

**Step 2: Facilitate Conversation**
- Bring them together (if appropriate)
- Set ground rules (respectful, solution-focused)
- Help them understand each other's perspectives
- Find common ground

**Step 3: Create Agreement**
- How will they work together going forward?
- What needs to change?
- How will they handle future disagreements?

**Step 4: Monitor**
- Check in with both
- Watch for improvement
- Address backsliding immediately

**When to Escalate:**
- If conflict is severe (harassment, hostility)
- If parties can't work it out
- If it's affecting team
- Involve HR if needed

## Q: What if my team lacks diversity and inclusion?

**A:** Take proactive steps to improve.

**Short-term:**
- Evaluate hiring practices (bias in job descriptions, interview panels)
- Broaden sourcing (different networks, diverse recruiting)
- Structured interviews (reduce bias)
- Diverse interview panels

**Medium-term:**
- Inclusive team culture (psychological safety, belonging)
- Mentorship and sponsorship for underrepresented groups
- Retention focus (exit interviews, stay conversations)
- Development opportunities distributed equitably

**Long-term:**
- Pipeline development (internships, partnerships)
- Company-wide D&I initiatives
- Leadership commitment
- Accountability and metrics

**Your Role as Manager:**
- Advocate for diverse candidates
- Create inclusive team environment
- Challenge bias when you see it
- Sponsor and advocate for underrepresented ICs
