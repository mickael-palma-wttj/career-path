# Resources and Support

Questions about finding help and resources when you're new to management or need guidance.

---

## Q: What if I'm new to managing and don't know how to develop ICs?

**A:** You're not alone—developing others is a learned skill.

**Immediate Steps:**

**1. Use This Handbook:**
Start with [Getting Started Guide](../MANAGER_HANDBOOK/00_Getting_Started.md)

**2. Learn the Framework:**
Understand [Career Framework](../MANAGER_HANDBOOK/01_Framework_Overview.md)

**3. Read Case Studies:**
Learn from real scenarios in [Case Studies](../CASE_STUDIES/)

**4. Use Templates:**
Leverage ready-to-use [Templates](../TOOLS_TEMPLATES/)

**5. Find a Mentor:**
Connect with experienced manager in your org

**6. Management Training:**
Ask your company for management training programs

**7. Read:**
- "The Manager's Path" by Camille Fournier
- "Radical Candor" by Kim Scott
- "An Elegant Puzzle" by Will Larson

**8. Practice:**
Start with 1:1s and career conversations. Get feedback. Iterate and improve.

**Remember:** Everyone starts as a new manager. Be patient with yourself while holding yourself to high standards.
