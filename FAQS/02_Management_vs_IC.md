## Q: Can someone switch back from management to IC?

**A:** Yes, and it's becoming more common and accepted.

**Reasons for Switch:**
- Realized management isn't the right fit
- Misses technical depth
- Life circumstances changed
- Management opportunity was temporary (interim role)

**How to Support:**

**If IC Returns to Your Team:**
- Frame positively: "Taking on leadership experience and bringing it back to IC work"
- No shame or stigma
