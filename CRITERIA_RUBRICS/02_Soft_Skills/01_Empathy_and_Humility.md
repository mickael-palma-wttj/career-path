## ğŸ¯ Definition

**Empathy & Humility** means:
- **Perspective-Taking** - Understanding how others see situations
- **Openness** - Genuinely considering feedback and different views
- **Growth Mindset** - Believing you can improve
- **Respect** - Valuing others' contributions and viewpoints
- **Self-Awareness** - Understanding your own impact on others

---

## ğŸ“Š Level-by-Level Breakdown

### Developing (0.0-1.0)
- Often defensive about feedback
- Dismisses different perspectives
- Difficulty seeing situations from others' views
- Blames external factors for mistakes
- Doesn't ask for others' input
- Learning that different is valid

### Competent (1.0-2.0)
- Generally accepts feedback (though may not like it)
- Can understand others' perspectives with effort
- Takes responsibility for mistakes
- Seeks input but doesn't always apply it
- Learning empathy

### Proficient (2.0-3.0)
- Genuinely interested in others' perspectives
- Actively seeks feedback and applies it
- Takes feedback professionally; learns from it
- Others feel heard by this person
- Shows respect for different views
- Admits mistakes and learns from them

### Expert (3.0-3.5)
- Deeply empathetic; others feel genuinely understood
- Seeks diverse perspectives proactively
- Mentors others in emotional intelligence
- Growth mindset very evident
- Creates psychological safety
- Respected for humility and openness

### Authority (3.5-4.0)
- Org exemplar of empathy and humility
- Shapes org culture toward empathy
- Others model their empathetic approach
- Creates environment where people thrive
- Known for character and integrity
